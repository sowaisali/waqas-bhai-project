About Me

I’m a Web Developer with a strong focus on creating responsive, user-friendly websites that are optimized for performance and scalability. I have a solid understanding of modern web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and various web frameworks, and I specialize in crafting websites that deliver seamless experiences across all devices.

I use best practices to improve website visibility and ranking on search engines, helping businesses increase their organic traffic and online presence. From keyword research to on-page SEO and link-building strategies, I work to ensure that every site I develop is not only functional but also discoverable by the right audience.

Whether it's building a website from the ground up or improving the search engine ranking of an existing site, my goal is to create digital solutions that are both technically sound and optimized for search success.
